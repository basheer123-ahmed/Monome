Our Foundation

Born from a Passion for Purposeful Design

MONOME Constructions was born from a deep conviction — that every building has the power to change lives. Founded in 2009, we began as a boutique architecture and interior design practice (Sila Architects & Furnco), driven by two brothers who believed that spaces should not just be functional, but profoundly human.

Over 15 years, that vision has evolved into a full-spectrum construction powerhouse. Today, MONOME Constructions delivers end-to-end design, engineering, and build services — uniting artistic excellence with cutting-edge construction technology.

The FutureReady™ Difference

Our exclusive FutureReady™ Homes philosophy integrates cutting-edge technology and transparency into every project — giving you complete peace of mind from foundation to finish.

Building Health Passport™

Complete transparency at every stage. Every material, every milestone, every inspection — documented and accessible in your personal building health record.

Digital Twin Model™

Your home, mapped for life. We create a precise digital replica of your structure — for future maintenance, renovation, or extension planning with zero guesswork.

Live Construction Tracking™

Real-time progress updates, anytime, anywhere. Track your build from anywhere in the world through our dedicated client dashboard — updated daily.

50-Year Serviceability Design™

Built for lasting strength and comfort. Our structural designs are engineered to remain functionally superior for 50+ years — without major retrofitting.

Transparent Cost Dashboard™

Clear costs, no hidden surprises. Our itemised cost dashboard gives you full visibility over every rupee spent — from materials to labour to finishing.

Dedicated Client Relationship™

Your personal project manager, available throughout design, construction, and post-handover — ensuring a seamless, stress-free building experience.
